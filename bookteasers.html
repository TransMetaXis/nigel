The Exalted Nature Books?

Simply put, it's a collection of poetry that spans from the years 2014-2019, ending before I began the visual novel trilogy. The first book Destined Crest took the longest out of the three due to the fact that I was unaware I was developing the potential for a book so I took my time. The second Adorned Road took much less time due to the coordinated method I would use Having nearly every poem identical in overall structure. The third, Bloomed Scope had to have taken the least amount of time since all the content was there in its raw form, all I had to do was convert the prose to poetry.
