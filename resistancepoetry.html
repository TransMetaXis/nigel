What Is My Resistance Poetry?

The following works are my creative expressions of sustained frustrations and near helplessness in how controlled the mass public became by the our corrupt governments, corporate mainstream media and the established, compromised scientific community. It amazed me how widespread censorship became during these times. Censorship of well regarded scientific professionals speaking truths that would result in less profits for those who had a vested interest in keeping the vaccines the only accessible treatment for the illness to fill their greedy pockets. An illness that pushed multiple doses despite the fact that the illness grew weaker over time with its mutations. These poems were my main method of getting my voice heard in such a way that wouldn't be altogether attacked since it was in poetry form. Sneaky on my part I know, but I felt at the time I had to do something, in my own way.

Public Safety

  • With all of us social distancing and ordered to wear masks
  • Something's been on my mind so I really have to ask
  • If I got the common cold with fever, sniffles and the rest
  • Would I test positive for a covid-19 test?
  • You don't hear much about the common cold these days
  • Lately everyone who's sick has covid so they say
  • Does it not make you wonder where the cold and flu have gone?
  • And if you bring that up you're told obey because you're wrong
  • Whenever I acquiesce to rules of constriction
  • I feel like I dishonor all the truth of my convictions
  • Remember that I've touched only on one point of this scam
  • There's also death rates vaccines hospitals the list goes on god damn
  • Perhaps you don't appreciate this poem not one bit
  • Well I try to help the veil to lift but if not so be it
