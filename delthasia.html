What Is Delthasia?

This is the first of the three visual novels of the Exalted Nature game series. Heavy on story, light on gameplay and medium on humor. The gameplay consists of linear exploration with a turn-based combat system. Some encounters with be avoidable, others not. Also there are points in which you must interact with the play screen to for instance open doors or activate objects. There is also a dialogue and morality choice system that appears in certain scenes

The story itself revolves around the unlikely hero Elzarus. A member of a sect of druidic healers meant to cleanse the land of darkness, injury or disease as well as being able to bring abundance and growth to the nature of the land of Delthasia. He is tasked by the gods and goddesses of the planet to travel the continent in search of the root cause of what is currently ailing Delthasia and bring her back to peace.

These three games were intended to be experienced first with Delthasia, then Vuldron and finally Aerde but since Vuldron is a prequel to Delthasia, one may desire to try the prequel first. Aerde however must be played last due to a lot of missing context that would spoil and confuse the player, just a disclaimer. I hope you enjoy the journey of this trilogy, five years in the making.
