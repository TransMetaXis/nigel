What Is Exalted Nature?

This site has been about ten years in the making though only recently did I give enough consideration in creating this creative expression hub for myself and my works.

About ten or so years ago, I took advantage of my gradually decreasing hypo-manic state from a previous loss of touch with reality by expressing myself through the art of poetry.

I didn't think much about it at the time until I had eventually garnered enough works to fill my first book Destined Crest and from then on I remained inspired to continue and complete the poetry trilogy.examples

What got me on the path of writing and developing the trilogy of games was during school when given a text adventure assignment. I came up with the story of Elzarus a sort of homage to the biblical figure Lazarus

And common in the writing process there were a lot of editing and revisions for all three games but the spirit of the outline for each of them remained and was honored through the final interpretations.
